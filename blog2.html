The BPC market in the age of internet economy

The BPC Market in the age of internet economy The emergence of e-commerce companies like Nykaa, Purplle and recently Myntra has given a strong fillip to the industry.
Beauty and Personal Care( BPC) are an integral part of an individual’s aesthetic and personality. Of course, there are many forms of beauty. If one is to search for it, one can find beauty in confidence. Loving oneself is also what makes one look beautiful to the world. But its also true that the same confidence of being beautiful and loving oneself can be derived from the usage of beauty and wellness products, which can make one feel good about themselves.

As Michelle Phan says and i truly believe-
“Make up is not a mask that covers your beauty; it’s a weapon that helps you express who you are from the inside.”

The easy availability of these genuine weapons in this internet age is what has been a big boon to women across India. At the forefront of this revolution has been NYKAA, founded by an experienced women entrepreneur, Mrs. Falguni Nayyar.

The cosmetic products always carry a grave risk of counterfeiting. The online platforms, like Nykaa, over time have gained our trust in offering genuine products. This is the single biggest checkpoint in Beauty products, which the new age internet companies shall pass through. But now Online fashion-store , Myntra has also taken a step further by pumping up its beauty and personal care portfolio and is ready to take Nykaa in this BPC market.Another incentive to shop BPC products online is the larger no. of options available. After all, the more the no of choices, the merrier is the experience.

With $250 million revenue in FY20, Nykaa accounts for over 30% of the online BPC market. India’s beauty and personal care category is estimated at $14-15 billion. It is expected to report an average annual growth of 8% in the next five years, according to market researcher Euromonitor.

Overall, the internet ecosystem has contributed to increased trust and loyalty in new age companies , while also opening up exposure to Premium and international brands, which are a huge plus for the consumers looking for convenient shopping exposure.
